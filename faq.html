Frequently Asked Questions

What is BTCMiner?

BTCMiner is an industry leading Bitcoin mining pool. All of the mining power is backed up by physical miners. Mining with the latest algorithms allows to make as much Bitcoin as possible. We aim to provide you with the easiest possible way to make money without having to do any of the hard stuff.

With data centers around the globe, we aim to keep bills down and mining power high, meaning you can make more in a shorter amount of time than what it would take to mine from your home for instance. Our data centers are located in Europe, USA and China with dedicated Up-Links and 99% uptime!

How do I start?

Sign up providing your wallet address and start generating Bitcoin.

I don't have a Bitcoin wallet. How can I create one?

You can create Bitcoin wallet using online services like Blockchain.info.

I don't have Bitcoin. Where can I get Bitcoin?

You can buy and sell Bitcoins on exchanges like LocalBitcoins.

How much can I earn without investments?

You can generate 0.0006 BTC every day without investments. You can also upgrade your BTCMiner to generate up to 0.5 BTC every day.

How much can I earn on the partnership program?

You will earn 20% to 100% every time your referral gets upgrade depending on your BTCMiner version. If you have BTCMiner V1.0 - your profil will be 20%, 30% for V1.1, 40% for V1.2, 50% for V1.3 and 100% for V1.4

Can I have multiple BTCMiner accounts?

You can have multiple V1.1 or higher Premium accounts, but only one V1.0 Free account.

How long does it takes to withdraw money?

Withdrawal applications are generally processed instantly, in rare cases withdrawals can be processed manually and take longer.

What is the minimum amount for withdrawal and upgrade?

Minimum withdrawal amount is 0.005 BTC. Minimal upgrade amount is 0.001 BTC.

If I pay for an upgrade how long is it active?

BTCMiner upgrades have no limits and works as long as our data centers are powered. Our uptime is 99% so upgrade confidently.

Can I upgrade BTCMiner with money on my balance?

BTCMiner currently does not support upgrading from internal BTCMiner wallet yet. You can only upgrade using external wallets.
